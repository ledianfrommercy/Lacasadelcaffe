About Us

Our Heritage

Nestled in the heart of Morris Park, a neighborhood rich with Italian heritage, La Casa Del Caffe has been serving the community with exceptional espresso since the late 1990s. Our founder, Anna Agovino, discovered her unique talent for espresso making quite by accident, but soon became the soul of our café.

Anna's signature touch and meticulous attention to every detail of espresso making have turned our modest café into a landmark for coffee lovers. Known for her magic wrist and the thick creamy head of her espresso, Anna's dedication ensures every cup is a testament to traditional Italian coffee making. It's not just coffee we're brewing; it's a legacy.

Commitment to Excellence

At La Casa Del Caffe, we pride ourselves on our commitment to sustainability and community involvement. Each espresso served is a celebration of rich flavors and the joy of Italian coffee, crafted with love and precision. Join us for a taste of history and experience the best coffee in New York City, as endorsed by our loyal patrons and connoisseurs alike.

Don't miss out on our weekly specials! Enjoy a buy-one-get-one-free offer on all espresso drinks this week. Plus, join us for our monthly coffee tasting event to explore unique blends from around the globe and meet fellow coffee enthusiasts.
